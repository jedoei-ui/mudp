Your Path

From first note
to full song

A clear, rewarding progression designed for lasting results.

01

Discovery Session

A relaxed first lesson where we explore your musical background, your goals, and what kind of pianist you want to become. No pressure — just music.

02

Foundations

We build strong technique, hand coordination, and reading skills through music you actually enjoy playing — not just exercises.

03

Repertoire & Expression

With solid foundations in place, we dive into full pieces — expanding your repertoire and developing your unique musical voice.

04

Performance & Mastery

Prepare for exams, recitals, or simply the joy of playing beautifully for an audience. This is where all the work pays off.

Hover a step to explore

01

Step One

Discovery Session

  • 60-minute relaxed conversation and play session
  • Explore your musical history and inspirations
  • Identify your goals — classical exams, favourite songs, or pure enjoyment
  • We design your personalised lesson plan before you leave
02

Step Two

Foundations

  • Posture, hand position, and injury-free technique from day one
  • Sight-reading introduced gradually at your pace
  • Basic chord theory woven into real songs you know
  • Both hands working together within the first few lessons
03

Step Three

Repertoire & Expression

  • Build a personal repertoire across genres you love
  • Dynamics, phrasing, and musical storytelling
  • Improvisation and composition introduced at this stage
  • Regular recordings to hear your own progress
04

Step Four

Performance & Mastery

  • AMEB, ABRSM, and Trinity exam preparation available
  • Bi-annual studio recitals in a supportive environment
  • Stage presence and managing performance nerves
  • Ongoing lessons to keep growing — there's always a next level
